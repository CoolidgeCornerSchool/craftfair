The Coolidge Corner School PTO presents...

Coolidge Corner School

Annual Crafts & Services Fair

When
Wednesday, January 30, 2019
6:00pm-7:30pm
Exhibitors should arrive to set up at 5:30pm
Where
Brookline Coolidge Corner School, in the cafeteria.
Note: Please enter through the Stedman St. door
Who
All Coolidge Corner students K-8 are welcome.
to sell their creations and/or services
What
Make unique crafts, delicious treats, or whatever you can think up.
Offer nail polishing, hair styling, tattoos, games, card trading, face painting, and more!
How much
Optional donation: $2.00 per crafter
To be collected at the door on the day of the fair.
All proceeds will be donated to the Brookline Food Pantry.

Registration deadline: Wednesday January 16

Remember, it must be homemade. Reselling store-bought items or food that you did not make is not allowed.
Please read the Craft Fair Tips for Success.

Questions? Contact Melissa Dulla or Christina Knopp.

Changes? Please contact Christina Knopp if a crafter has already registered and has a change to make.
