Devo Science Fair

Saturday March 12, 2016

Devotion School, Brookline, MA

Theme

This year's theme is Earth and the Environment, but projects are welcome to be on any science topic you like.

Schedule

8:30amRegistration/Set up opensThe cafeteria will be open so students can set up their projects.
9:00-12:00Science Fair/Science Expo The science fair opens to the public.
At the science Expo, enjoy a New England Aquarium touch tank, exhibits, and activities presented by professional scientists.
9:00-11:30Judging timeThe student participants present their projects to the judges, view the other projects and visit the activity expo.
12:00-12:30Award ceremony

How to make a great science fair project

  • Find any topic that interests you
  • Ask a question
  • Make a prediction
  • Do an experiment to answer your question
  • Write down the results
  • Make a poster to share your project
